## About Argument AI

Argument AI is a high-performance, real-time interview assistant designed to help professionals master their technical and behavioral interviews. By combining advanced audio capture with cutting-edge AI, our tool provides:

- **Real-time Audio Processing**: Captures and processes interview dialogue with ultra-low latency.
- **Intelligent Insights**: Delivers instant, AI-powered suggestions and answers to help you stay ahead.
- **Comprehensive Reports**: Seamless integration with our web dashboard for session reviews and performance tracking.
